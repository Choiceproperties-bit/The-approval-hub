Is this service legal and ethical?

100%. We operate in full compliance with all fair housing and consumer protection laws. We are a verification and tenant advocacy service. We help you present a complete, accurate, and verifiable picture of your suitability as a tenant.

What is your guarantee?

Our Total Turnaround Package comes with our 'Approval Guarantee.' If you follow our plan and are not approved for a qualifying property within 90 days, you will receive a 50% refund. Individual services have a 95% success rate.

How soon can you help me?

Our Rental Verification and Co-Applicant services typically take 24-48 hours to set up. The Total Turnaround Package requires a more comprehensive approach but shows results within the first week.

Do I have to talk on the phone?

Your comfort is our priority. Our entire process is designed to be handled asynchronously via our secure client portal and email. You can message your specialist directly without a single phone call.
